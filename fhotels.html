TOP 5 HOTELS

  1. Galle Face Hotel (Colombo, Sri Lanka)

    Established in 1864, this hotel combines colonial-era grandeur with modern luxury. It has hosted iconic figures like Mahatma Gandhi and Richard Nixon and offers stunning seafront views along with amenities like a museum and croquet lawn.

  2. Hotel del Coronado (California, USA)

    Opened in 1888, this Victorian-style beach resort is a National Historic Landmark. It's famous for its architecture, celebrity guests, and appearances in films like Some Like It Hot. Visitors enjoy a blend of luxury and nostalgia.

  3. Hôtel de Paris Monte-Carlo (Monaco)

    A palatial retreat since 1864, this hotel has hosted royalty, celebrities, and socialites. It features suites with views of the Monaco Grand Prix track and pays tribute to Princess Grace Kelly.

  4. Grand Hotel (Mackinac Island, Michigan, USA)

    Known for its world-record porch and Victorian elegance, this hotel has been a retreat since 1887. It offers a taste of historical charm with modern luxuries like a grand pool and activities such as biking.

  5. Hotel Windsor (Melbourne, Australia)

    Established in 1883, this Victorian-style hotel has preserved its original features while hosting famous guests like Katharine Hepburn and Sir Laurence Olivier. Its opulent suites provide views of the Parliament House and Treasury Gardens.
